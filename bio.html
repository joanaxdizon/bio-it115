About me

Joana is a recent graduate of the University of Washington, where she completed a Bachelor of Science in Public Health-Global Health. During her time in college, she was part of a professional health fraternity, a student-led editorial, the campus' peer health educators, as well as numerous other leadership committees. Joana is currently in her first quarter of the Year Up program, where she hopes to learn how to leverage technology to improve health systems and address health disparities in marginalized communities.
